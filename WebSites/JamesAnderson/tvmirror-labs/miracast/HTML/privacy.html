Privacy Policy for Smart TV Mirroring

TVMirror Labs Co,.Ltd explains how information is handled when you use Smart TV Mirroring.

Last Updated:

TVMirror Labs Co,.Ltd ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Smart TV Mirroring application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We may collect certain information when you use our App, including:

  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access our App, including device model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: We collect data about how you interact with our App, including mirroring preferences and frequently accessed content.
  • Network Information: To establish connections between devices, we may collect information about your local network configuration.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App's functionality

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Troubleshoot technical issues

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in operating our App (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • When required by law or to respond to legal process

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
